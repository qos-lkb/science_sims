原子電離！ IONIZATION
提示：光子必須具有特定的共振能量才能被原子吸收，否則會直接穿透。

能量吸收與激發

氫原子中的電子平時處於能量最低的「基態」($n=1$)。當外界粒子與其碰撞時：

  • 光子碰撞： 必須具備「精確」的能量差（$\Delta E = E_f - E_i$）才會被吸收。誤差容許度極低，能量不符則直接穿透（透明）。
  • 電子碰撞： 屬於非彈性碰撞。只要入射電子動能大於能階差，就能將能量傳遞給原子使其躍遷，剩餘動能繼續由入射電子攜帶。

電離作用 (Ionization)

當電子獲得的總能量足以使其能量大於 $0$ 時，電子將脫離原子核的束縛成為自由電子。對於光子，若能量大於電離能（光電效應），也能造成電離。

註：若電子已在 $n=2$ ($-3.4\text{ eV}$)，只需額外 $3.4\text{ eV}$ 即可電離。

1913年，尼爾斯·玻爾提出三個著名公設，成功解釋了氫原子光譜。模型結合了經典力學的圓周運動與量子化的概念。

一、定態公設 (Stationary States)

電子在特定的軌道上繞核運動時，具有固定的能量，且**不會**輻射電磁波。

$$r_n = n^2 \cdot a_0 \quad (a_0 \approx 0.529 \text{\AA})$$

二、頻率條件 (Frequency Condition)

躍遷時吸收或放出的光子能量恰等於兩能階之差：

$$\Delta E = |E_f - E_i| = h\nu$$

三、角動量量子化 (Angular Momentum)

電子繞核運動的角動量 $L$ 只能是 $\hbar$ 的整數倍：

$$L = mvr = n\hbar \quad (n=1, 2, 3 \dots)$$

Energy Absorption & Excitation

The electron in a Hydrogen atom usually resides in the lowest energy "Ground State" ($n=1$). When external particles collide with it:

  • Photon Collision: Must have the exact energy difference ($\Delta E = E_f - E_i$) to be absorbed. If the energy doesn't match, it passes through (transparent).
  • Electron Collision: This is an inelastic collision. As long as the incident kinetic energy is greater than or equal to the energy level difference ($\ge \Delta E$), it can transfer energy to the atom causing a transition, retaining the remaining kinetic energy.

Ionization

When the electron gains enough total energy to exceed $0\text{ eV}$, it escapes the nucleus's binding to become a free electron. For a Hydrogen atom, ionizing from the ground state requires at least $13.6\text{ eV}$.

Note: If the electron is already at $n=2$ ($-3.4\text{ eV}$), only an additional $3.4\text{ eV}$ is needed to ionize.

In 1913, Niels Bohr proposed three famous postulates that successfully explained the Hydrogen spectrum. The model combines classical mechanics' circular motion with the concept of quantization.

1. Stationary States

Electrons orbit the nucleus in specific orbits with fixed energy without radiating electromagnetic waves. These orbits are called Stationary States. This solved the classical problem where accelerating charges should radiate energy.

$$r_n = n^2 \cdot a_0 \quad (a_0 \approx 0.529 \text{\AA})$$

2. Frequency Condition

When an electron transitions between energy levels $E_i$ and $E_f$, it must absorb or emit a photon of a specific frequency, where the energy equals the difference between the levels:

$$\Delta E = |E_f - E_i| = h\nu$$

3. Angular Momentum Quantization

The angular momentum $L$ of the electron orbiting the nucleus can only be integer multiples of $\hbar$ ($h/2\pi$). This is the core assumption of the model:

$$L = mvr = n\hbar \quad (n=1, 2, 3 \dots)$$
